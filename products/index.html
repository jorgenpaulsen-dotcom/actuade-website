Product catalogue

Subsea pan & tilt units

Actuade subsea pan and tilt units are designed for precise positioning of ROV cameras, sonar packages, sensors and lights in subsea and underwater environments. The range includes compact and high-torque underwater pan tilt units in pan/tilt and pan-only configurations for inspection, monitoring and underwater camera positioning applications.
