Actuade AS

Norwegian precision for global subsea operations.

Actuade AS is based in Stjørdal, Norway and designs and manufactures Norwegian subsea equipment, including subsea pan and tilt units for ROVs, sonar systems, monitoring packages and instrument platforms.

Our story

Built with operators.

The SPT series was developed from real operational needs: fewer connector failures, higher impact tolerance and predictable movement under load.

Actuade works closely with end users, subsea operators, integrators and field technicians to keep product development grounded in the conditions equipment actually faces offshore.
