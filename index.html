Why Actuade

Designed around real subsea failure modes.

01

No rotating connectors

Cabling is routed internally, eliminating one of the most common subsea failure points.

02

High-mounted rotation

The rotation axis is positioned to handle impact loads, thruster wash and exposed subsea mounting points.

03

Ethernet / Modbus UDP

Ethernet with Modbus UDP, RS-485 and RS-232 support makes integration straightforward across vehicle, skid and instrument control systems.

Field validated

Engineered for long subsea service.

Actuade products are developed with subsea operators, integrators and field technicians, then engineered for hard use: oil-filled gearboxes, precision strain wave gears, absolute position feedback and customer-selectable connectors.
