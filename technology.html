Design philosophy

Reliability before everything else.

Subsea operations often run around the clock. Component failure means suspended work, vessel time and expensive recovery. Actuade designs for real offshore constraints and long service intervals.

The SPT series combines internal cable routing, high-mounted rotation, oil-filled gearboxes and configurable communication interfaces in a compact subsea actuator for camera systems, sensors and sonar packages.

Design features

Nine decisions that matter in the field

01

6000 m rated

Pressure equalised design keeps operation predictable from shallow water to deepwater deployments.

02

High-mounted rotation

The rotation axis helps distribute impact loads away from the gear train.

03

Absolute feedback

12-bit absolute encoders report position at all times with approximately 0.1 degree resolution.

04

No rotating contacts

Internal cable routing removes a common electrical failure point from the rotating assembly.

05

Strain wave gears

Low backlash supports precise camera, sonar and instrument positioning under load.

06

Oil-filled gearboxes

Oil-flooded gear chambers provide lubrication and pressure compensation support.

07

Material options

Hard anodized aluminium as standard, with titanium available for applications with demanding corrosion requirements.

08

Connector choice

Customer-selectable connector options help match existing vehicle, skid and instrument architecture.

09

Ethernet / Modbus UDP

Ethernet with Modbus UDP support sits alongside RS-485 and RS-232 for modern subsea control systems.

Operator-led development

Designed with the people who recover the equipment.

Actuade products were developed in close cooperation with subsea operators, integrators and field technicians. The result is equipment that prioritises structural integrity, integration flexibility and uptime.
