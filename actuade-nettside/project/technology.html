Design Features

Nine engineering decisions that matter.

01
6000 m Rated

Oil-filled construction with external compensation provides pressure equalisation across the full 6000 m depth range — no internal pressure management required.

02
High-Mounted Rotation

The rotation axis sits high on the unit, distributing impact loads away from the gear train. Withstands direct thruster wash without mechanical degradation.

03
Absolute Position

12-bit absolute encoders report position to ~0.1° at all times. No homing routine required on power-up — the system knows where it is immediately.

04
No Rotating Connectors

Rotating electrical connectors are one of the most common subsea failure modes. The SPT series routes cabling internally — eliminating this failure point entirely.

05
Precision Strain Wave

Strain wave (harmonic drive) gears deliver backlash under 3 arc minutes — providing the repeatable, precise positioning that inspection and intervention tasks require.

06
Minimal Downtime Design

External compensation and connectorisation simplify inspection and maintenance. The design minimises time off the water — not by making field repair easy, but by making failures rare.

07
Hard Anodized Aluminium

Hard anodized 6000-series aluminium as standard. Titanium available on request for applications requiring superior corrosion resistance or reduced in-water weight.

08
Oil-Filled Gearboxes

Oil-flooded gear chambers provide lubrication across the full depth range and act as an additional pressure compensation buffer — extending service intervals significantly.

09
Ethernet Native

Full Ethernet support alongside RS-485 and RS-232 — enabling direct integration with modern ROV control architectures without protocol converters.

Design Philosophy

Engineered for work-class ROVs.

Work-class ROV operations run 24/7 in demanding offshore environments. Every component failure means suspended operations — costs that dwarf the value of the equipment itself.

The SPT series is built around this reality. Not the minimum viable product, but the most reliable one.

Robustness over weight

The high-mounted rotation axis and solid material cross-sections prioritise structural integrity in high-current, high-impact environments.

Designed with operators

The SPT design evolved through close co-operation with ROV operators and field technicians — real requirements, real solutions.

Configurable for every application

Pan & tilt or pan-only, aluminium or titanium, Ethernet or serial — and customer-selectable connectors throughout.
