115 Series.

SPT-115 · SPA-115 · SPT-315 · SPA-315

Designed for continuous, hard use in the most demanding subsea environments. Developed in close cooperation with ROV operators, the 115 Series withstands thruster wash and physical impacts thanks to its high-mounted rotation axis and precision strain wave gears.

  • SPA-115 — Pan only, aluminium
  • SPT-315 — Pan & Tilt, titanium
  • SPA-315 — Pan only, titanium

150 Series.

SPT-150 · SPA-150 · SPT-350 · SPA-350

The heavy-duty model for high-payload applications. With 50 Nm of torque and a dual-yoke capacity of 100 kg, the 150 Series handles large cameras, sonar heads, and heavy intervention tooling. The titanium variant — SPT-350 — offers reduced weight and superior corrosion resistance.

Configurations
  • SPT-150 — Pan & Tilt, aluminium
  • SPA-150 — Pan only, aluminium
  • SPT-350 — Pan & Tilt, titanium
  • SPA-350 — Pan only, titanium
  • Ethernet, RS-232 or RS-485
