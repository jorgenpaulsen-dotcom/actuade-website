Our Story

Norwegian precision for global subsea operations.

Actuade AS is a Norwegian manufacturer of high-quality subsea components, based in Stjørdal. Our products are developed in close co-operation with end users and validated through extensive field testing.

The SPT series was built from the ground up to address real operational failures experienced by ROV crews — particularly the high rate of connector and gear failures caused by thruster proximity and mechanical impact.

Our approach: design for the worst case, manufacture to the highest standard, and keep the unit easy to service in the field.
