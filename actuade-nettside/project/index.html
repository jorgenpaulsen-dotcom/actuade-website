Why Actuade

Designed for the hardest conditions.

01
No Rotating Connectors

One of the most common subsea failure points eliminated entirely. Cables route internally — no rotating electrical contacts.

02
High-Mounted Rotation

The rotation axis is positioned high on the unit to withstand impacts and thruster wash — a critical advantage on work-class ROVs.

03
Ethernet Native

Full Ethernet support alongside RS-485 and RS-232 — direct integration with modern ROV control systems, no protocol converters.
